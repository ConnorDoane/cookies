Baking Steps!

  1. Mix the Cookie Dough and Chocolate Chips in the Large Mixing Bowl
  2. Place Small Mounds of Cookie Dough onto the Cookie Tray and place the Tray in the Oven
  3. Bake at 450 degrees Farhenheight for 20 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
